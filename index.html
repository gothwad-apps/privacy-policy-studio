Last Updated: January 2026

App Name: GX Studio (formerly Gothwad Studio)

Welcome to GX Studio. This application is developed and managed by Gothwad Apps Pvt. Ltd. ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: When you register (via Google Login or Email), we collect your Name, Email Address, and Profile Picture to create your unique user identity.
  • User Generated Content (Projects): The code, files, and projects you create within GX Studio are stored securely on our cloud servers (Firebase) so you can access them from any device.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (model, OS version) to improve app stability.

2. Third-Party Services (Firebase)

GX Studio uses services provided by Google Firebase. By using this app, you also agree to Google's privacy practices. We use Firebase for:

  • Authentication: To securely log you in and manage your account.
  • Cloud Firestore: To store your projects, chat messages, and user profile data.
  • Analytics (Optional): To understand how the app is used and improve performance.

3. Communication & Community Features

⚠️ Important regarding Global Chat:
GX Studio features a "Global Chat" and "Community" section. Please be aware that any message, code snippet, or information you post in the Global Chat is public and can be viewed by all other users of the application. We advise against sharing sensitive personal information (like phone numbers or passwords) in public chats.

We also provide AI-assisted coding features ("Vibe Coding"). Data sent to the AI is processed solely for generating code suggestions and is not stored permanently for other purposes.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the GX Studio IDE service.
  • To sync your projects across devices.
  • To facilitate communication between developers in the Global/Personal Chat.
  • To detect and prevent bugs or fraudulent activities.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (provided by Google Firebase) to protect your data. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

GX Studio is a coding tool intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this from our servers.
